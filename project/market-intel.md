# Tri-Cities Housing Market Intel (Johnson City / Kingsport / Bristol)

**Timestamp: 2026-03-11 07:35 EDT**

## Snapshot (Feb 2026 data unless otherwise noted)
- **Median sale price:** $448,950 in the Tri-Cities, +6.8% vs. Jan and +3.2% YoY; third-highest monthly median on record, just below the July 2022 peak of $450,490. (Source: March 2026 market update, Cari McGee — carimcgee.com)
- **Days on market:** 42 median days; this is the third straight month above 40 DOM, signaling buyers still take their time while demand stays steady. (Same source)
- **Inventory:** Active listings ticked up 1% month-over-month, rose to 1,000+ homes, and are +29% vs. last March; a rare early-spring inventory bump means buyers have more choices before the usual seasonal drop. (Same source)
- **Affordability mix:** 44% of Feb sales landed between $160K–$299,999 (90 sales in the $200K–$249,999 band), while the move-up tier ($300K–$499,999) accounted for 31% of volume. (Same source)
- **Balance cue:** East Tennessee as a whole is hovering between 3–5 months of inventory, so the Tri-Cities’ slight inventory rise keeps the market around a balanced (not overheated) posture. (East Tennessee outlook, katelyntnrealtor.com)
